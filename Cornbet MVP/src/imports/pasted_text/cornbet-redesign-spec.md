Product Concept

CornBet is a group betting system where friends bet against a shared bank instead of a sportsbook.

Each player starts with $500 in play balance.

The group bank starts with $1000.

Rules:

When a user loses a bet, the money goes into the Group Bank

When a user wins, the payout goes to their Play Balance

The group bank will later be converted to Bitcoin for DraftHaus 2026

This concept should be explained subtly in the UI.
